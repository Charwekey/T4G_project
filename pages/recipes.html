Avo Eggs Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (whole-grain or sourdough)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes or parsley for garnish (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden
    brown and crispy.
  2. Cut and mash the avocado in a bowl,
    seasoning it with salt, pepper,
    and optional chili flakes.
  3. Heat a pan with butter or olive oil,
    and cook the eggs (fried, poached,
    or scrambled as desired).
  4. Spread the avocado mash evenly on
    the toast, and top each slice with an egg.
  5. Garnish with chili flakes or parsley
    and serve immediately.

Chicken tacos

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Chicken Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 8 small taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • ½ cup salsa
  • ½ cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
  • Lime wedges (optional)

Procedure:

  1. In a skillet, heat the shredded
    chicken and mix it with taco seasoning.
  2. Warm the taco shells in the oven
    or microwave.
  3. Fill each shell with seasoned chicken,
    lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream.
  4. Serve with lime wedges for an
    extra burst of flavor.

Lava Cakes

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Lava Cakes

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • ½ cup dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder or powdered sugar (for dusting)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C)
    and grease ramekins with butter.
    Dust with cocoa powder.
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate together
    over a double boiler or microwave.
  3. Whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar in a
    bowl until pale and frothy.
  4. Fold in the melted chocolate and sift in
    the flour. Mix until smooth.
  5. Divide batter into ramekins and bake for
    10–12 minutes until the edges
    are set but the center is soft.
  6. Let cool for 1–2 minutes before inverting
    onto a plate.
    Serve with whipped cream or ice cream.

Strawsilade

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Strawsilade

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 4–5 fresh basil leaves
  • 1–2 tablespoons sugar or honey
  • 1 cup lemonade (or sparkling water)
  • Ice cubes
  • Extra basil leaves and strawberries for garnish

Procedure:

  1. In a pitcher, muddle the strawberries,
    basil leaves, and sugar/honey.
  2. Add lemonade or sparkling water and
    stir well.
  3. Pour over ice cubes in glasses and
    garnish with basil leaves and strawberry slices.

Baked Ziti

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Baked Ziti

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ziti pasta
  • 2 cups marinara or pasta sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef or sausage
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Cook ziti according to package
    instructions and drain.
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground
    beef or sausage, then add
    marinara sauce and Italian seasoning.
  3. In a baking dish, layer the cooked
    pasta, ricotta, meat sauce,
    and mozzarella.
  4. Repeat the layers and top with
    Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20–25
    minutes until bubbling and golden.
    Garnish with parsley before serving.

Thai Green Curry

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Thai Green Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken or tofu, cubed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, carrots)
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (or soy sauce for vegetarian)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Fresh basil leaves

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry the
    green curry paste for 1–2 minutes
    until aromatic.
  2. Add coconut milk and stir well.
    Bring to a simmer.
  3. Add chicken or tofu and cook for
    5–7 minutes.
  4. Toss in vegetables, fish sauce, and
    sugar. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve hot with jasmine rice, garnished
    with fresh basil leaves.

Spinach Pie

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Spinach and Feta Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup ricotta or cream cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Sauté the spinach in a pan until wilted.
    Let cool.
  3. In a bowl, mix spinach, feta, eggs,
    ricotta, salt, and pepper.
  4. Roll out puff pastry and place it in
    a baking dish.
    Fill with the spinach mixture.
  5. Fold edges of the pastry over the filling
    and bake for 25–30 minutes
    until golden.

Shakshuka

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Shakshuka

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 4–6 eggs
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet.
    Sauté onion, garlic, and bell
    pepper until softened.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, paprika,
    and cumin. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Make small wells in the sauce and
    crack the eggs into them.
  4. Cover and cook until the eggs are
    set (about 5–7 minutes).
  5. Garnish with parsley or cilantro
    and serve with crusty bread.

Quiche Lorraine

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Quiche Lorraine

Ingredients:

  • 1 pie crust
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (Swiss or Gruyère)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt, pepper, and nutmeg to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    Place the pie crust in a tart pan.
  2. Sprinkle bacon and cheese evenly
    over the crust.
  3. Whisk eggs, cream, salt, pepper,
    and nutmeg. Pour over the filling.
  4. Bake for 30–35 minutes until the
    filling is set and golden.

Pesto Pasta Salad

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Pesto Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 300g (about 2 cups) pasta
    (fusilli or penne works well)
  • ½ cup pesto sauce
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup fresh mozzarella balls or
    diced mozzarella
  • ¼ cup black olives, sliced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package
    instructions. Drain and let cool.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the pasta with
    pesto sauce, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and
    olives (if using). Toss to combine.
  4. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and
    serve chilled or at room temperature.

Minestrone Soup

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Minestrone Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (400g) cannellini or kidney beans, drained
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • ½ cup small pasta (like ditalini)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or Parmesan cheese for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot.
    Sauté onion, garlic, carrot,
    and zucchini until softened.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, beans, vegetable stock,
    and oregano. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add pasta and simmer until tender,
    about 10 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
    Serve hot with parsley or
    Parmesan cheese.

Garlic Chicken

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • Green onions for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix honey, garlic,
    soy sauce, and olive oil.
  3. Place the chicken in a baking dish
    and coat with the honey garlic mixture.
  4. Bake for 30–35 minutes, basting
    occasionally with the sauce.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
    Serve with rice or steamed vegetables.

Gnocchiin Sauce

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Gnocchi in Cream Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 package (500g) gnocchi
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Cook the gnocchi according to the
    package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, melt the butter and
    sauté the garlic for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
    Stir in Parmesan cheese.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, and toss
    the gnocchi in the sauce.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.

Eggplant Parmesan

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Eggplant Parmesan

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into rounds
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Olive oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the eggplant slices with salt
    and let sit for 10 minutes to remove excess moisture.
    Pat dry with a paper towel.
  3. Dip each slice into the beaten eggs,
    then coat with breadcrumbs.
  4. Fry the slices in olive oil until
    golden brown and set aside.
  5. In a baking dish, layer marinara sauce,
    eggplant slices, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
    Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  6. Bake for 20–25 minutes until bubbling
    and golden. Serve hot.

Chili Con Carne

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Chili Con Carne

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Brown ground beef in a large pot
    and drain excess fat.
  2. Add onion and garlic, sauté
    until softened.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, kidney beans,
    chili powder, and cumin.
  4. Simmer for 20–30 minutes.
    Adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. Serve with rice, tortilla chips,
    or sour cream.
